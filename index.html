Safety, compatibility and notes

Web Bluetooth requires a user gesture to connect and a secure origin. Only one app can usually hold the BLE connection. Disconnect the official Sphero app first. Test with R2-D2 lifted off the floor or with plenty of space before enabling drive/raw motor commands. Do not drive while the charging cable is attached.

This page implements public reverse-engineered R2-D2/Sphero V2 commands. Some firmware revisions may reject, ignore, or time out on undocumented commands. The raw sender is included so you can extend or test additional command IDs without editing the app.
